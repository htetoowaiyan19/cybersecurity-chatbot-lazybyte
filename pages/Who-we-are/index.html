Company History - LazyByte

LazyByte was founded in 2023 with a bold mission: to make digital security smarter, simpler, and more accessible through the power of artificial intelligence.

What began as a small research initiative between cybersecurity enthusiasts and machine learning experts has grown into a trusted platform for secure user experiences.

From our early days building phishing detection tools, LazyByte quickly evolved. By combining AI, real-time threat analysis, and legal compliance standards, we developed an ecosystem that not only defends against cyber threats but also educates users and organizations.

In 2024, we launched our first AI-powered chatbot, transforming how individuals interact with cybersecurity. By the end of that year, our tools were integrated by small businesses, educators, and IT teams seeking smarter, user-friendly protection.

Our mission continues: to protect people through intelligent, ethical, and responsible technology.
